Import your Moosend Landing Pages into WordPress with just one click!

== Description ==

## Getting started

1. [Register with Moosend](https://www.moosend.com)
2. Install the plugin in your website
3. Login with your Moosend credentials in the authentication page.
4. Go to the importer page and start importing your Moosend landing pages into your WordPress website.

## Publish your Moosend Landing Pages on your Wordpress Site

Downloaded by each and every user that desires a flawless tool, this Moosend plugin for WordPress ensures that you can create, design, manage and publish your Moosend landing pages from your Wordpress site.

Inside the Moosend platform you can find a powerful landing page designer with features to build fully customizable, on-brand landing pages. Moosend’s landing page designer combines modern design, simple Call-to-Actions and leads to detail-perfection for desktop and mobile devices.

## Ready-made Templates For Beautiful Landing Pages On The Spot

Guide users to take action with professional, stylish pre-designed templates that align with your offer. Save time and get started with fully customizable templates and add a personal touch to get the most out of your campaigns.

## Looking Good On Any Device

Do mobile right. Create unified experiences from beginning to end regardless of the device they’re using. Just switch from desktop to mobile view, tweak your elements and create landing pages that don’t just “work” for mobile.

## Creating Urgency With Countdown Timers

Your offer isn’t going to be around forever. Add a countdown timer to your landing pages to create urgency and scarcity and generate more leads and sales. Simply drag and drop the countdown widget anywhere you want and leave the rest on their fear of missing out.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**Why do I need this plugin?**

Because it will allow you to import your Moosend Landing Pages into your WordPress website with just one click!

**Do I need to know how to code to use it?**

No, you only need to click on the blue Import button and that's it.

== Installation ==

1. Install using the WordPress built-in Plugin installer, or extract the zip file and drop the contents in the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress installation.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. After you have activated the plugin navigate to Moosend -> Authentication and from that screen fill the login form.
